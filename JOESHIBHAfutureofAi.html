YOU MAY NOT REALIZE IT, BUT AI IS HERE. FUTURE OF AI IS THE FUTURE OF ALL

by K.JOESHIBHA

From leading players like Amazon Alexa, Apple Siri and Microsoft Cortana adding brand new features to  deliver superior customer experience to the explosion of chatbots revolutionizing customer interactions. Machine Learning is a significant part of the future of Artificial Intelligence.  In 2020, Machine Learning will continue to play a game- changing role across various fields that will extend beyond sales and marketing.  Microsoft announced the general availability of Azure Machine learning service, which is all set to simplify and accelerate the process of developing and deploying Machine learning models.  The U.S. Army uses Machine learning to predict when the fighter vehicles would need maintenance or repair.  Google has integrated AI to predict patient's deaths and promises a staggering 95% accuracy in results, which is higher than the in-house warning systems at hospital. New tool of AI  Researchers from Harward University and the MIT-IBM Watson AI lab have developed a new tool for spotting text that has been generated using  AI called the Giant Long Model Test Room(GLTR) it exploits the fact that AI text generators reply on statistical pattern in text, as opposed to the actual meaningof words and sent.  In other words, the tool  can tell if the words you're reading seem too predictable to have been writtenby a human hand.  GLTR  highlights words that are statistically likely to appear after the preceding word in the text.  The most predictabe  words are green, less predictable words are yellow and red and least predictable on purple.  When  tested on snippets of text written by Open AI's algorithm, it finds a lot of predictability.
